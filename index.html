Top 10 Machines of the 2000s

The 2000s were peak stupid: high-rev Hondas, sketchy Mustangs, and insurance-fraud WRXs. These are the only machines that mattered. No beige appliances. No crossovers. If it doesn’t make noise and bad decisions, it’s not on this list.
